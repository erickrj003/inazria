## Kelanari

The Kelanari are a proud and ancient race dwelling within the Violet Sea itself, between the Kystriland Isles and the Inazrian coast. These aquatic humanoids possess an innate connection to their underwater territory, viewing themselves as guardians of the ocean's mysteries. Kelanari society values artistic expression through sculpture and song designed to be experienced best among the currents of the Violet Sea. Their cities are carved into coral reefs and sea cliffs with flowing architecture that mimics the movement of currents.

Kelanari are tall and graceful, standing between 6 and 7 feet in height, with lean, muscular builds adapted for swimming. Their skin ranges from deep blue-green to pale aquamarine, and includes subtle scale patterns along their arms and legs. Distinctive gill slits along their necks close when on land, and their webbed fingers and toes make them exceptional swimmers. Their large eyes are adapted for underwater vision and come in shades of deep blue, green, or silver. Most Kelanari have flowing hair in blues, greens, or silver-white that moves like kelp in water.

Generally peaceful, Kelanari are famously politically neutral. They do not interfere with the conflicts of land-dwelling men unless there is a threat to their own waters. They generally view land-dwellers with caution, understanding the value of outside contact but preferring the safe domain of their waters. For practical reasons, very few outsiders ever get to experience Kelanari lifestyles. Their long lives - often reaching 250 years - give them patience that can frustrate younger races, but also wisdom that proves invaluable in times of crisis.

### Traits

- **Ability Score Increase**: +2 Constitution
- **Age**: Kelanari mature by age 30 and can live just over 250 years.
- **Size**: Kelanari are tall and lean. Your size is Medium.
- **Speed**: Your base walking speed is 25 feet. You have a swimming speed of 40 feet.
- **Amphibious**: You can breathe both air and water.
- **Darkvision**: Adapted to the ocean depths, you can see in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light, and darkness as if it were dim light, but only in shades of blue and green.
- **Natural Swimmer**: You have proficiency in Athletics checks related to swimming.
- **Storm Sense**: You can predict weather changes up to 24 hours in advance with perfect accuracy.
- **Languages**: You can speak, read, and write Common and Kelanari. Kelanari is a flowing, musical language that incorporates tonal elements mimicking whale songs and ocean currents.

### Kaduran

The Kaduran make their homes in sea caves and tidal pools around the smaller Kystriland islands. Diplomatic and adventurous, Kaduran are the likely Kelanari seen on land living full lives amongst the other races. They are master craftspeople, creating intricate coral jewelry and sculptures that are highly prized by traders. Their settlements are semi-aquatic with features like tide pools and channels to bring the sea directly into their homes.

- **Ability Score Increase**: +1 Charisma
- **Language**: You can speak Kadurani, a mutually-intelligible dialect of Kelanari which incorporates many Kystri trade terms.
- **Coral Crafting**: You have proficiency with jeweler's tools and gain double proficiency bonus when working with coral, pearls, or sea-based materials.
- **Tidal Attunement**: REWORK You always know the current tide level and can predict tidal changes, giving you advantage on Navigation checks in coastal waters.

### Pelagoran

The Pelagoran live aboard great floating cities that drift with the currents of the Violet Sea. These wandering Kelanari are master navigators and known to guide their settlements away from areas they predict will be hit by storms. They are known for their epics, songs which are sung by entire communities in unison, that can be heard for miles across the water and sometimes used for communication and to guide lost travelers to safety.

- **Ability Score Increase**: +1 Wisdom
- **Language**: You can speak Pelagorani, a highly unique language relying on various tones and clicks. It is common for different Pelagoran groups to have many differences in their language. Due to the many sounds required, it is a difficult language for non-Kelanari to learn.
- **Storm Calling**: Once per long rest, you can cast gust of wind as a 1st-level spell without using a spell slot.
- **Master Navigator**: You have proficiency with navigator's tools and can never become lost at sea, barring magical effects. You have advantage on Survival checks while on or near large bodies of water.

### Khemazi

The mysterious Khemazi dwell in the deepest parts of the Violet Sea, in cities built within underwater trenches and abyssal plains. These Kelanari have adapted to crushing depths and eternal darkness, developing enhanced senses and a more reserved, contemplative culture. They are scholars of the deep ocean's secrets and guardians of ancient knowledge.

- **Ability Score Increase**: +1 Intelligence
- **Language**: You can speak Khemazi, the ancient language from which modern Kelanari derives from. To outsiders, the language is poetic and formal.
- **Abyssal Adaptation**: You take no damage from water pressure and can survive at any depth.
- **Deep Sight**: Your darkvision extends to 90 feet, and you can see normally in complete darkness underwater.
- **Lack of Storm Sense**: Khemazi do not benefit from the Storm Sense racial ability.
