## Elves

Avarazhan's elves are best defined by their meekness, mystical artistry, and otherworldly beauty. Their culture is as charming as it is mysterious; few outsiders are ever blessed to experience their arcane ways. The Elves admire nature, magic, and pageantry, devoting much of their unusually long lives towards achieving unimaginable feats in their crafts. Elves see beauty in many things, particularly in nature and the earth that surrounds them. An elf's perception of intellectual ideas, such as psychology and sociology, often come from long-lived experience. Slow to strong emotional responses, elves tend to remain aloof and flexible amidst the unpredictabilities of life.

Elves are characteristically deft and nimble. Elves are shorter than humans but taller than dwarves, often below 5 feet tall up to around 6 feet in height. Their lean, wiry frames typically weigh in-between 100 to 145 pounds. The dichotomy of sizes between men and women are quite minor; a brother may not be much taller or heavier than his sister, if at all.

Elven society famously exists amidst remote environments far away from the other races. Out of respect for nature, elves are usually self-sustaining hunter-gatherers, and typically do not desecrate their lands by clearing and plowing it. Elves from these rural communities don't travel regularly, but an elf's long life often leads to wanderlust somewhere down the road. Sometimes, elves will immerse themselves into the culture of other men, likely out of curiosity, though famous examples of cross-cultural zealots exist. These elves who thrive in the cities often ascend to high leadership roles. An elf committed to fighting the good fight undoubtedly brings incredible martial and magical skills to any fortunate ally.

### Traits

- **Ability Score Increase**: +2 Dexterity
- **Age**: Although elves reach physical maturity at about the same age as humans, the elven understanding of adulthood goes beyond physical growth to encompass worldly experience. An elf typically claims adulthood and an adult name around the age of 100 and can live to be 750 years old.
- **Size**: Elves range from under 5 to about 6 feet tall and have slender builds. Your size is Medium.
- **Speed**: Your base walking speed is 30 feet.
- **Darkvision**: You have superior vision in dark and dim conditions. You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.
- **Fey Ancestry**: You have advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can't put you to sleep.
- **Trance**: Elves don't need to sleep. Instead, they meditate deeply, remaining semiconscious, for 4 hours a day (The Common word for such meditation is "trance."). After resting in this way, you gain the same benefit as 8 hours of sleep.
- **Elf Weapon Training**: You have proficiency with the longsword, shortsword, shortbow, and longbow.
- **Languages**: You can speak, read and write Common and Elvish. While the language between Elvish clans differ in significant ways (and are not mutually-intelligible with each other), modern simplified Elvish combines the common dialects for speaking to foreign traders and other Elves. Standard Elvish is always written in the Common script. Regardless, the Elvish languages are lyrical and poetic, supplemented by thousands of years of Elvish music and chronicles.

### Veldtwin

Veldtwin elves are the most common kinds of elves. Their exceptionally thin, athletic builds and keen senses allow them to move naturally amongst the massive trees, groves and thickets of the Veldt, Avarazhan's impressively dense and tall forests. Although the veldtwin elves, colloquially called wood elves, inhabit a wide space of land, they do so in small communities hidden away from the average traveler's path. Veldtwin elves are curious and clever, often leaving their homes to join the rest of the world for years at a time before retreating to their temperate recluse. In fact, many wood elves get along so well with the outsiders that they are frequently found permanently residing among them. The veldtwin elves exhibit physical colors similar to Inazria's men, but more often include shades of copper, darker brown, or red. Their eyes are between green, brown, or bright hazel tones.

- **Ability Score Increase**: +1 Charisma
- **Language**: You can speak Veldtwin. Veldtwin words are short and consonant-heavy, resulting in a "choppy", lighthearted sound. Their script is simple and quickly written.
- **Fleet of Foot**: Your base walking speed increases to 35 feet.
- **Mask of the Wild**: You can attempt to hide even when you are only lightly obscured by foliage, heavy rain, falling snow, mist, and other natural phenomena.

### Sarukhwin

Sometimes called silver elves or rock elves, the Sarukhwin of western Avarazhan's plateau region are a shrewd group particularly gifted in the arcane arts. These sagacious elves spend their long childhoods devoted to magic, particularly when it comes to the elements and astronomy. Unlike other elves, rock elves' emphasis on logic translates to a culture built on law and order. The more rigid and intellectually focused lifestyles of the rock elves often cause them to be perceived as somewhat arrogant or hubristic, unwarranted or otherwise. Rock elves typically have silver, bronze, or darker skin tones, with hair of black, blondish-white, red, or dark brown shades. Their eyes can be silver, gold, or hazel colors. Rock elves are slightly taller than other elves on average, and not quite as thin as their Veldtwin cousins.

- **Ability Score Increase**: +1 Intelligence
- **Language**: You can speak Sarukhwin, a complex language developed over several centuries. The script is very intricate and must be carefully thought-through, but once finished is a work of art. Spoken Sarukhwin involves many sounds.
- **Astrological Attunement**: Whenever you make an Intelligence (Arcana) check, you are considered proficient in the Arcana skill and add double your proficiency bonus to the check, instead of your normal proficiency bonus.
- **Cantrip**: You know one cantrip of your choice from the Magi spell list.

### Tsythdaruu

Inhabiting the far eastern tundras, the rare Tsythdaruu (white elves) have existed as small, close-knit family groups for centuries. Generations of enduring some of the most difficult terrain in the world has developed capable, careful hunters. The white elves, sometimes known as tundra elves or snow elves, are known to be prudent and fiercely independent - if not a little bit stubborn. Despite this, white elves are famously empathetic and hospitable to wanderers ill-equipped to traverse the wastelands. Snow elves are thorough thinkers and naturally skilled at discerning the morality of others. They rarely have the time or motive to travel far into urban society. The relatively shorter, stockier white elves of the frozen far southeast exhibit blue or copper undertones throughout their sheet white or gray skin. Their hair comes in black, silver, mint green or bluish-white with little in between, and eyes resonating light blues, greens, grays and hazels.

- **Ability Score Increase**: +1 Wisdom
- **Language**: You can speak Tsyth. It is a highly unique language with very little in common to the other Elvish languages. Traditionally, the Tsyth language had no script, but has recently adopted the Common script for official purposes.
- **Winter Resistance**: You're naturally adapted to cold climates, as described in chapter 5 of the Dungeon Master's Guide. Additionally, you gain resistance from cold-based damage.
- **Detect Empathy**: Any Intimidation, Deception, or Persuasion check made in bad faith against you is at a disadvantage.

### Sindibaan

Scattered across the northern Kystriland islands and parts of northern Inazria live the Sindibaan, a group of elves known for their agriculture, carefree attitudes, and strong history of martial mercenaries. Commonly called night elves by other Inazrian natives, the Sindibaan are responsible for orchards and farms that produce much of the world’s flavorful crops. Physically, the Sindibaan are distinguished by their robust builds and deep purple, blue-black, or rich brown skin tones that seem to shimmer slightly under moonlight. Their eyes are small and thin, ranging from deep violet to silver, and their hair is typically black, deep blue, or silver-white. 

- **Ability Score Increase**: +1 Strength
- **Language**: You can speak Sindibaanese, a straightforward dialect of modern Elvish influenced by vocabulary from the ancient form of Sindibaan and the form of Common spoken by north Inazrians. 
- **Mercenary Heritage**: Choose any two-handed martial weapon. As per Sindibaan warrior tradition, you have proficiency with that weapon and gain a permanent +1 attack bonus when using it in combat.
- **Agreeable**: Your disposition is lighthearted and easygoing, encouraging others to trust you easier. You gain proficiency in the Persuasion skill.
