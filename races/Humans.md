## Humans

The Humans of Inazria are perhaps the most diverse and adaptable of all the races, spread across the tropical peninsula from the great capital of Thilidan to the remote fishing villages along the coast. Humans have built a civilization that values innovation, trade, and cultural exchange. Human society is marked by its rapid pace of change and remarkable capacity for both great achievement and devastating conflict. The human race truly exists everywhere - even in the most remote, extreme or unlikely places in the world.

Humans are the most numerous race in Azergad, and their shorter lifespans create a sense of urgency that drives them to accomplish great things in relatively brief periods. They are natural traders and diplomats, serving as bridges between the other races through their adaptability and openness to new ideas. Human settlements range from the sprawling metropolis of Thilidan to small farming communities, each reflecting the local culture and environment.

Physically, humans are highly variable but typically stand between 5 and 6 feet tall with all kinds of lean and stocky builds. Their skin tones vary from pale to deep brown, with hair colors spanning the full spectrum from black to blonde. Humans mature by age 18 and typically live 70-90 years, though some exceptional individuals may reach 100 and beyond.

### Traits

- **Ability Score Increase**: +1 to two different ability scores of your choice. At 10th level, you may add +1 to another ability score.
- **Age**: Humans mature by age 18 and live up to 90 years.
- **Size**: Humans vary widely in height and build. Your size is Medium.
- **Speed**: Your base walking speed is 30 feet.
- **Versatile**: You gain proficiency in one skill of your choice.
- **Determined**: You can add your proficiency bonus to one ability check that doesn't already include your proficiency bonus. You can use this feature once per long rest.
- **Languages**: You can speak, read, and write Common plus your choice of trade language: Kystri, Veldtwin, or Gotoro.
